All about Froggy!

The Model

FroggyLoch’s model perfectly captures her playful, fae-inspired charm. Her main design features soft pink skin, shoulder-length pink hair styled in buns with a lively ahoge, and delicate green wings that highlight her mischievous, otherworldly nature. She often accessorizes with frog-themed elements, like a lily pad crown or froggy eye details, reflecting her deep love for amphibians. Her 3D model, which she helped design and texture herself in collaboration with SapakiJoe, showcases her artistic skills and strong personal branding.

The Fugi

FroggyLoch’s Fugi (a reactive PNG model used for streaming) captures her adorable, mischievous vibe in a simple but charming way. It typically features her as a cute frog or her pink-haired fae persona, often with big expressive eyes and playful poses that react when she speaks. Even in this minimal form, the Fugi design shows off her love for frogs and her whimsical, friendly energy, making her presence on stream feel lively and personal.

The Friends

FroggyLoch is known for her warm, supportive friendships and frequent collaborations within the indie VTuber and art communities. She often works with fellow creators who share her love of cozy, whimsical, and creative vibes, including other artists, riggers, and VTubers who help bring her playful ideas to life. Her collaborations feel genuine and fun, whether she’s working on music, art projects, or chaotic group streams. These friendships not only highlight her friendly and approachable personality but also strengthen the tight-knit, encouraging atmosphere that her fans love about her online presence.
